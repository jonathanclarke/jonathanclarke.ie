After a week of travel in Bangkok, I returned with a slightly unusual souvenir: a case of sore shins courtesy of a deep tissue massage.

Running was out of the question the past week - my legs needed movement, not impact.  So, I swapped the treadmill for the indoor bike to get a workout without stressing the shins.

I kept the resistance steady and focused on turnover for a full hour.  It was a "Manual" session designed to get the blood flowing and the joints moving again.

**The Data:**

- Time: 1:00:01
- Distance: 21.92 km
- Average Speed: 21.9 km/h
- Average Heart Rate: 100 bpm
- Calories: 405

It felt good to be moving again. The heart rate stayed in a perfect recovery zone (100 bpm), and I banked nearly 22km of distance while letting the legs heal.  Onwards to another day.
